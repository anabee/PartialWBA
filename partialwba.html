Unemployment Made Easy
Unemployment benefits are available to employees who are out of work temporarily, through no fault of their own. Most people who collect unemployment have lost their jobs. However, it is possible to be eligible for benefits even if you are still working, if your hours or pay have been cut or you have been forced to take a part-time position and you can't get additional work. The calculator below can help you estimate the partial weekly benefit amount you may be eligible for.

  1. Enter the wages you were receiving weekly before your hours or pay were reduced.
  2. Enter the reduced weekly wages you are now receiving.
  3. Select the state in which you intend to file.
